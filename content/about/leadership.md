# Leadership Team

US Micro Products is led by a team of display industry veterans who combine deep technical expertise with decades of supply chain and business experience. Our leaders have worked across every major display technology generation — from monochrome character LCDs through today's high-resolution AMOLED and advanced TFT platforms — and bring that breadth of knowledge to every customer engagement.

In a company of approximately 30 people, leadership isn't isolated in a corner office. Our executives work directly with customers, participate in design reviews, and stay connected to the engineering and operational details that determine project success.
