# How Can We Help?

US Micro Products provides engineering-focused support throughout your display project — from initial technology selection through production and long-term lifecycle management. Find what you need below, or [contact us directly](/contact) to talk with an engineer.

## [Frequently Asked Questions](/support/faq)

Answers to common questions about ordering, lead times, minimum quantities, technical specifications, customization options, and our design process. If you're evaluating US Micro Products as a display partner, start here.

## [Request a Quote](/support/request-quote)

Ready to get pricing on a display solution? Submit your requirements — including quantities, target application, and timeline — and our team will respond with a detailed proposal. Custom projects include NRE estimates and preliminary timeline.

## [EOL & Obsolete Parts](/support/eol-parts)

Display discontinued by your current supplier? We help OEMs find replacement displays for end-of-life and obsolete parts. Cross-references, form-fit-function replacements, and redesign support to keep your product in production.

## [Contact Us](/contact)

Reach our Austin, Texas headquarters by phone, email, or contact form. Office hours, directions, and international office information.
